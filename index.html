1. IP and Licensing Risks

Dependence on third-party IP creates vulnerability. License renewals can be expensive or lost to competitors. Building owned IP takes time and has high failure rate.

2. Market Saturation

Collectors have limited budgets and shelf space. Too many releases can lead to fatigue. Competition intense across price points. Funko decline (-4% YoY) shows saturation risk.

3. Trend Dependency

Consumer preferences change quickly. What is hot today may be irrelevant tomorrow. Requires constant innovation and fresh IP. Blind box trend may not sustain 107% growth rates.

4. Manufacturing and Supply Chain

Most production concentrated in China. Quality control critical for premium products. Supply chain disruptions can delay launches. Counterfeits erode brand value.

5. Economic Sensitivity

While collectibles show resilience, discretionary spending can decline in recessions. Premium segment ($100+) more vulnerable than mass market. Unemployment impacts collector community spending power.
